About

Hey there! My Name is Elizabeth, I am a current student at Southeast Community College in the Graphic Design Media Arts Program. This is my website of my work, about myself, and how to get ahold of me.

I really like doing web design, I didn’t enjoy it the first time but this time I really do love it. I also like making logos and doing invitations for anyone’s party needs.

I first feel in love with graphic design from my cad teacher because I really enjoyed cad but it was a lot of math but he introduced me to graphic design and when I was his teacher assistant he would give me graphic design stuff to work on whether that be invitations or logos just stuff to look through and come up with new things.

Look around and Enjoy!
